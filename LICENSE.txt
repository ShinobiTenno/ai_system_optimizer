About Alpha-1 COPD:
Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency (Alpha-1) is a genetic form of COPD that affects both the lungs 
and liver. Unlike typical COPD, Alpha-1 can affect people regardless of smoking history or age. 
Early detection and treatment are crucial for managing this condition. Learn more at:
https://www.alpha1.org/newly-diagnosed/learning-about-alpha-1/
